Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Perfect Soup.

General

What equipment do I need?

A measurement microphone (any calibration mic works), an audio interface with the mic input and speaker outputs on the same device, and a computer running Windows 10+ or macOS 11+.

How many speakers are supported?

From stereo to 7.1.4 Atmos and beyond. Depending on your license tier, you can have 2 to unlimited speaker groups, each with its own primary and support speakers.

Does it work with subwoofers?

Yes, subwoofer integration is a core feature. Perfect Soup optimizes the crossover between main speakers and subs, aligning phase and timing for seamless bass response.

Licensing & Pricing

What is a “support”?

A support is a supporting speaker within a speaker group. For example, in a stereo pair with a subwoofer, the subwoofer counts as one support.

Can I upgrade later?

Yes. Perpetual license holders can upgrade to a higher tier anytime. Use the Upgrade button on any higher-tier card.

What about calibration?

Perpetual licenses include 1 year of calibration access. Rent-to-own plans include calibration as part of the monthly fee. Standalone calibration extension is available for $49 for 30 days or $69 for 365 days.

Do I get free updates?

Yes. All license holders receive free lifetime software updates for as long as Umami Audio continues to develop Perfect Soup.

Refund policy

You can request a full refund within 14 days of purchase (or any longer period required by consumer-protection law in your country) by emailing support@umamiaudio.com. We recommend using the free 60-day trial first to confirm the product and system requirements fit your setup.

License transfer

Perpetual licenses can be transferred to another user for a $20 fee.
