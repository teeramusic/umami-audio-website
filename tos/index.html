1. Definitions

Perfect Soup
The complete room calibration suite by Umami Audio, comprising the Calibration Wizard and Calibration Loader.
Calibration Wizard
The standalone application used to measure room acoustics and generate calibration files.
Calibration Loader
The DAW plugin (VST, AU, AAX) that loads and applies calibration in your session.
Support
A supporting speaker within a speaker group. For example, in a stereo pair with a subwoofer, the subwoofer counts as one support.
Tiers
License levels that determine limits on speaker groups and supports per group.

3. VST License Terms

  • Perpetual: One-time purchase; use indefinitely subject to these terms.
  • Rent-to-own: Monthly billing that includes calibration access while active. After 24 monthly payments the license automatically converts to a permanent perpetual license at no further charge. Cancelling stops billing and your progress toward ownership; access continues until the end of the paid period and the license does not convert. There is no annual plan.
  • Free trial: Each new account is granted one free 60-day trial of The Perfect Soup tier; it expires automatically and requires no payment.
  • You may install the Calibration Loader on unlimited computers, but only one computer may actively use it at a time (i.e., the plugin must be instantiated and not bypassed on only one machine).
  • Offline grace period: 7 days (may be adjusted per license at Umami Audio's discretion).

4. Calibration Subscription

  • An active calibration subscription is required to create new calibrations.
  • Perpetual tiers include bundled calibration time: 1 year for main tiers, 1 month for support add-ons. Extensions stack.
  • Standalone calibration extension: $49 for 30 days or $69 for 365 days, for perpetual license holders who need to extend beyond the bundled period.
  • Rent-to-own plans include calibration as part of the monthly fee while active; on conversion to perpetual, the standard 1-year bundled calibration applies.
  • Existing calibrations remain usable after your calibration subscription expires; you may not create new calibrations until you renew.
